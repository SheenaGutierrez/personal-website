MY FAMILY

My source of strength!

"Everyone needs a house to live in, but a supportive family is what builds a home"

In today's world where everything is losing its meaning, we need to realize the importance of family more than ever. While the world is becoming more modern and advanced, the meaning of family and what stands for remains the same.

Family helps us to get through the most disastrous times and the best times. Family is important because they can offer support and security coupled with unconditional love. They will always look to see and bring out the best in you even if you cannot see it for yourself.

Family is also very crucial to guide you down the path of morality when it comes to outside influences such as dealing with some negative things. Your family might not be able to shield you from everything, but they will be able to assist you through the hard times and give you tools to avoid some situations.

My Friends

They are my Senior Highschool Friends

"A real friend is one who walks in when the rest of the world walks out."

Friends will come and go in your life. No matter how long your friendship last, the most importatnt thing is your friend's acceptance of you for who you are. A good friend walks the talk and shows that they care by their actions- big and small.

Living life without having the experience of friendship is like living without a soul. Human interaction is important for survival in our society, but developing frienships is crucial to the success of any person. That is why friendship is important and why we should appreciate this sacred relationship.
